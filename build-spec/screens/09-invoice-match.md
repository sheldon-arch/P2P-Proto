# Screens: 09 Invoice Capture and Matching (Build-real)

Flow 09 realized as screens. Source: `documentation/09-invoice-match.md`, data dictionary `Invoice.json` / `MatchResult.json` / `MatchException.json` / `GrIrEntry.json` / `CreditDebitNote.json` / `PaymentMilestone.json`, `model/role-permission-matrix.md`, state machine `matchLifecycle`. Covers invoice capture with duplicate detection, the two-vs-three-way match workbench, typed exception routing and resolution, GR/IR clearing, and the SoD-gated invoice approval.

### S09.1 Invoices to Match (archetype: queue / per-role landing for Finance-Maker)
- **Route:** `/invoices`
- **Roles:** Finance-Maker (act: `invoice.capture`, `invoice.view`, `match.run`); Buyer (view, assemble package, `invoice.capture` conditional); Fin-Checker (view, `invoice.view`).
- **Realizes:** entry point; one half of the Finance-Maker landing from storyboard section 6 (invoices to match + payment schedule due).
- **Purpose:** the AP maker's home - captured invoices and where each sits (on-hold duplicate, matched, exception, approved, released, rejected), so the maker can capture, run the match, and clear the queue. Answers "what is waiting on AP and what is stuck" in the first 30 seconds.
- **Data shown:** per invoice from `Invoice.json` - identifier, invoiceNumber, supplierId, poId, invoiceDate, totalInclTax, totalInclTaxInBase, currency, status (Badge); the match status from `MatchResult.json` (matchType, matchStatus Badge) and any open exception type from `MatchException.json` (Badge); a duplicate-hold Badge when status = on-hold.
- **Primary actions:** "Capture invoice" (-> S09.2; `invoice.capture`); "Run match" (-> S09.3 / `matchLifecycle` runMatch; `match.run`); row click -> invoice detail.
- **Secondary actions / navigation:** faceted filter by status / matchType / exception type / supplier; search by invoice number / PO / supplier; link to the payment schedule (flow 10).
- **States to design:** empty ("No invoices captured."), loading (skeleton table), populated, error.
- **Shadcn components:** DataTable, Popover+Command (faceted filter), Input (search), Badge (status, matchType TWO_WAY/THREE_WAY, matchStatus, exception type, duplicate hold), Button, Toast.
- **Acceptance criteria (believable done):** (1) the matchType Badge shows THREE_WAY when a GRN exists for the PO line and TWO_WAY when none does (axiom A8 visible at a glance); (2) an on-hold duplicate row is visibly held and cannot proceed to payment; (3) the exception-type Badge names the typed exception (price-variance / qty-over / qty-under / missing-GR / duplicate-invoice / tax-mismatch); (4) totalInclTaxInBase shows the FX-normalized figure with a "rate unavailable" note on FX failure (graceful degradation, never blocks).
- **Tags:** [S2.7 | AB invoice package + RA queue]

### S09.2 Capture Invoice (archetype: form)
- **Route:** `/invoices/new`, `/invoices/:id/edit`
- **Roles:** Finance-Maker (act: `invoice.capture`); Buyer (assemble package on the supplier's behalf, `invoice.capture` conditional); Supplier (submits via the external portal, scoped to own records - see `supplier-portal.md`).
- **Realizes:** steps 2-4 (submit invoice + documents, capture in AP and normalize to base, duplicate detection).
- **Purpose:** capture the supplier invoice into AP with its document package, normalize the tax-inclusive total to base currency, and screen for duplicates before any match runs; the originating AP record the payable depends on.
- **Data shown / captured:** `Invoice.json` - identifier (auto), invoiceNumber (the supplier's own reference; part of the duplicate key), supplierId (from PO), poId (resolves to an open PO; the match anchor), invoiceDate, currency (default PO currency), lines (array of {poLineId, description, quantity > 0, unitPrice >= 0, taxCodeId, taxableAmount, taxAmount}), amount (computed = sum line taxableAmount), taxAmount (computed = sum line taxAmount), totalInclTax (computed = amount + taxAmount), totalInclTaxInBase (FX-converted), reverseCharge (conditional imports), documents (array; invoice mandatory; packingList conditional goods; certificateOfOrigin/awbBl/bayan conditional import; healthCert conditional food RM import; dgDeclaration conditional dangerous goods), captureDate (auto).
- **Primary actions:** "Capture" (status = captured; `invoice.capture`; runs the duplicate check on the supplier + invoiceNumber + amount key; a match sets status = on-hold and shows the duplicate banner, else proceeds to match-type selection).
- **Secondary actions / navigation:** add/remove lines; attach documents; resolve poId to an open PO; the document-completeness checklist marks conditional documents per goods/import/food/DG.
- **States to design:** empty (new), loading (edit fetch), validation-error (inline per field + lines reconcile to total), duplicate-detected banner (supplier + invoiceNo + amount), captured toast.
- **Shadcn components:** Form (react-hook-form + Zod from `Invoice.json`), Sheet (line editor), Select/Combobox (poId, currency, taxCodeId), file Input (documents), Alert (duplicate banner), Badge, Button, Toast.
- **Acceptance criteria (believable done):** (1) a coincident invoice (same supplierId + invoiceNumber + amount as an existing invoice) sets status = on-hold and shows a duplicate-detection hold banner, and no payable is created while the hold is open (the standard AP control, duplicate key = supplier + invoice number + amount); (2) amount, taxAmount and totalInclTax are computed from the lines and reconcile (amount = sum taxableAmount, total = amount + tax); (3) totalInclTaxInBase is FX-normalized and degrades to the original unconverted amount on FX failure (never throws); (4) the invoice document is always mandatory and the conditional documents are required per goods/import/food RM/DG (document-completeness check); (5) reverseCharge is set for imports so the tax reconciliation expects buyer-self-accounted tax.
- **Tags:** [S2.7 | ISO 7.5 | AB invoice package + data-model DUPLICATE-INVOICE]

### S09.3 Match Workbench (archetype: workbench)
- **Route:** `/invoices/:id/match`
- **Roles:** Finance-Maker (act: `match.run`); System computes the match; resolvers act on exceptions from here (links to S09.4).
- **Realizes:** steps 5-11 (GRN-exists gate, milestone acceptance, two-way match, three-way match, within-tolerance gate, auto-clear + GR/IR relief, exception routing).
- **Purpose:** the marquee AP screen - a side-by-side reconciliation of PO, GRN (if any) and invoice that decides two-way vs three-way by GRN existence, applies the tax-inclusive tolerance on price/quantity/amount, auto-clears within tolerance (relieving GR/IR), and raises typed exceptions routed to the right resolver when outside.
- **Data shown:** three panes - PO (agreed price, lines, tax), GRN (`GoodsReceipt.json` receivedLines accepted quantity; present only on the three-way path), Invoice (`Invoice.json` lines, totalInclTax). The match config from `MatchResult.json` - matchType (THREE_WAY iff grnId set, else TWO_WAY), tolerancePricePercent, toleranceQtyPercent, toleranceAbsolute, taxInclusive (true), expectedTotalInclTax (three-way = GRN accepted qty * agreed price + tax; two-way = PO value incl. tax), matchStatus. Per-leg variance: pricePercent = abs(invoiceUnitPrice - poAgreedPrice)/poAgreedPrice; qtyPercent against GRN accepted qty (three-way) or PO qty (two-way); amountVariance tax-inclusive. For service POs the milestone pane from `PaymentMilestone.json` (acceptanceStatus must be accepted).
- **Primary actions:** "Run match" (`matchLifecycle` runMatch; within all bands -> matchStatus = matched, auto-clear, sets the `GrIrEntry` invoiceId/clearedAmount and status cleared or partially-cleared, posts the payable to the creditor ledger; outside any band -> matchStatus = exception with an exceptionReason, raises a typed `MatchException` routed by type; `match.run`, System); "Confirm milestone acceptance" (Engineering, two-way service path; acceptanceStatus = accepted; gates the two-way clear).
- **Secondary actions / navigation:** highlight the failing leg; open the raised exception (-> S09.4); on the three-way path show the GR/IR entry being relieved; a partial three-way clear leaves the GrIrEntry partially-cleared with the remainder clearing on the next matched invoice.
- **States to design:** loading, populated (matched - green, auto-cleared), exception (the failing leg highlighted, the typed exception raised and routed), two-way (no GRN pane), service (milestone pane), error.
- **Shadcn components:** bespoke three-pane comparison Table (PO / GRN / Invoice) with the best/expected value highlighted, Badge (matchType, matchStatus, per-leg pass/fail), Select (tolerance config read), Button (Run match), Toast.
- **Acceptance criteria (believable done):** (1) the match runs THREE_WAY when a GRN exists for the line and TWO_WAY when none does, decided by GRN existence not category name, and the three-way quantity is compared against GRN accepted quantity (not ordered quantity); (2) all comparisons are tax-inclusive - a price within band with a tax error still does not auto-clear and routes to Tax/Compliance; (3) within all three bands (pricePercent, qtyPercent, absolute) the match auto-clears, relieves the GR/IR (three-way) or posts directly (two-way), and posts the net payable to the creditor ledger; (4) outside any band a typed MatchException is raised and routed (price -> Buyer, qty-over/qty-under/missing-GR -> Receiving, tax-mismatch -> Tax/Compliance, duplicate-invoice -> Finance-Maker), never silently accepted; (5) a service PO with no GRN clears two-way only after milestone acceptanceStatus = accepted (milestone acceptance stands in for the GRN, axiom A8).
- **Tags:** [S2.7 | ISO 8.6 | data-model match logic + build-new GR/IR]

### S09.4 Resolve Exception (archetype: workbench / modal)
- **Route:** `/match-exceptions/:id`
- **Roles:** Buyer (price-variance, `match.resolveException` conditional price); Receiving (qty-over/qty-under/missing-GR, `match.resolveException` conditional qty); Tax/Compliance (tax-mismatch, `match.resolveException` conditional tax); Finance-Maker (duplicate-invoice hold + reject, `match.resolveException`).
- **Realizes:** steps 11-18 (route to resolver, buyer/receiving/tax/duplicate resolution, resolution outcome, post credit/debit note, return invoice to supplier).
- **Purpose:** the single screen where the accountable resolver sees the variance and closes the exception with one of the resolution set, re-running the match or adjusting the payable via a note or rejecting the invoice.
- **Data shown / captured:** `MatchException.json` - identifier, type (read), routedTo (read; the accountable role), variance (the out-of-tolerance amount/percent detail), resolution (Select {accept, adjust, credit-note, debit-note, reject}), resolvedBy, resolutionNote (mandatory for a duplicate false-positive override, recommended on accept/reject), linkedNoteId, resolvedAt. For credit/debit-note resolutions the `CreditDebitNote.json` sub-form - type (credit supplier-issued / debit buyer-raised), amount, taxAmount, reason, linkedInvoiceId, linkedMatchExceptionId.
- **Primary actions:** "Resolve" with the chosen resolution - accept or adjust (`matchLifecycle` resolveException -> re-runs the match; Buyer/Receiving/Tax-Compliance); credit-note or debit-note (posts a `CreditDebitNote` then clears; Finance-Maker raises, the adjusted net clears GR/IR or posts directly); reject (`matchLifecycle` resolveException -> REJECTED; returns the invoice to the supplier with the reason, no payable; Finance-Maker). For the duplicate hold the effective set is reject (confirmed duplicate) or accept (false positive, mandatory resolutionNote, re-route to match).
- **Secondary actions / navigation:** for missing-GR, link to raise the GRN (flow 08) or confirm the line is two-way; view the source match (S09.3); a corrected invoice re-enters at the match-type gateway.
- **States to design:** loading, populated (per type, only the routed resolver can act), resolution-selected (the credit/debit-note sub-form appears for note resolutions), duplicate-hold (reject / accept-with-reason only), resolved toast, invoice-returned toast.
- **Shadcn components:** multi-pane (variance detail + resolution), Select (resolution), Textarea (resolutionNote), Sheet (credit/debit-note sub-form), Badge (type, routedTo), Button, Toast.
- **Acceptance criteria (believable done):** (1) each exception type is actionable only by its routed resolver (price -> Buyer, qty/missing-GR -> Receiving, tax -> Tax/Compliance, duplicate -> Finance-Maker), enforced by permission, not just filtered; (2) accept and adjust re-run the match against the same three-way/two-way logic, while credit-note and debit-note adjust the payable via a note (the same mechanism a return uses) then clear, and reject returns the invoice with no payable; (3) a confirmed duplicate never posts a payable and a coincidental duplicate (false positive) requires a mandatory resolutionNote to override and re-route to match; (4) a credit/debit note sets linkedMatchExceptionId and the adjusted net then clears GR/IR (three-way) or posts directly (two-way); (5) a missing-GR exception is resolved by raising the GRN (flow 08) or confirming the line is two-way, and the corrected invoice re-matches.
- **Tags:** [S2.7 | ISO 8.6 | data-model MatchException routing + resolution set]

### S09.5 Approve Invoice (archetype: detail / modal)
- **Route:** `/invoices/:id/approve`
- **Roles:** Approver (act: `invoice.approve`, PO-head approve); Buyer (`invoice.approve` conditional, not if receiver).
- **Realizes:** steps 19-21 (approve invoice before accounts with SoD, release approved payable to payment).
- **Purpose:** the operational control before accounts - the matched (or note-adjusted) invoice is approved by someone who is not the receiver of the goods, then released as a net payable to payment.
- **Data shown / captured:** `Invoice.json` - the matched lines, totalInclTax, totalInclTaxInBase, the match result and any note adjustment, status; invoiceApprovalDecision (Select {approve, reject}), approvedBy (set on approve). The receiver of the goods is shown from `GoodsReceipt.raisedBy` for the SoD check.
- **Primary actions:** "Approve" (sets approvedBy, status = approved, then released to payment flow 10; `invoice.approve`; condition approver != the goods receiver and a buyer who received may not approve - SoD axiom A6); "Reject" (returns the invoice to the supplier; status = rejected, no payable).
- **Secondary actions / navigation:** view the match result (S09.3); view the GR/IR cleared for three-way lines; link to the released payment schedule (flow 10).
- **States to design:** loading, populated, permission-denied (the goods receiver sees no Approve action on the same invoice - SoD), approved toast/redirect, rejected toast.
- **Shadcn components:** Card + Tabs (Lines / Match / Documents), Dialog (approve/reject with decision), Badge (status), Button, Toast.
- **Acceptance criteria (believable done):** (1) the goods receiver (the GRN raisedBy) sees no Approve action on the same invoice and a buyer who received the goods may not approve it (SoD enforced, not just hinted, axiom A6); (2) only a matched or note-adjusted invoice can be approved (an open exception or duplicate hold blocks approval); (3) approval sets approvedBy and releases the net payable (after any note and withholding) to payment, clearing GR/IR for three-way lines; (4) a rejected invoice returns to the supplier and posts no payable; (5) this invoice-approval SoD is distinct from the maker/checker payment-release SoD in flow 10 (operational control vs financial control).
- **Tags:** [S2.7 | ISO 8.4 | AB invoice-package approval + role-matrix SoD]

### Inline edge behaviours shown on these screens (not separate screens)
- **Duplicate-invoice detection (e12):** shown inline on S09.2 as the hold banner on the supplier + invoiceNumber + amount key; no payable while the hold is open; resolved on S09.4 (reject confirmed / accept false-positive with a mandatory reason).
- **Two-vs-three-way by GRN existence (A8):** shown inline on S09.1 (matchType Badge) and S09.3 (the GRN pane present only on the three-way path); decided by GRN existence, never category name.
- **GR/IR clearing:** shown inline on S09.3 as the GrIrEntry being relieved on a matched three-way result; a partial clear leaves it partially-cleared with the remainder clearing on the next matched invoice; the two-way path has no GR/IR and posts the payable directly.
- **Currency fallback (e07):** shown inline on S09.1/S09.2 as a "rate unavailable" note on totalInclTaxInBase when FX fails; the original unconverted amount drives thresholds (graceful degradation, never blocks).
- **Tax reconciliation:** shown inline on S09.3/S09.4 - the match is tax-inclusive, reverse-charge is flagged for imports, and a tax error routes to Tax/Compliance even when price is within band.
